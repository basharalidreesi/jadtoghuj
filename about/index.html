--- title: About layout: Welcome to my digital world ! I am a self-taught fashion stylist and creative director. I began my styling career by experimenting on myself with different types of garments and silhouettes and on my own body. Through a process of learning through deliberate experimentation, I grew a keen fondness for editorial styling. Throughout the past three years, I have collaborated with many Jordanian designers and international brand names and participated in a number of local fashion events. I usually find inspiration in electronic and alternative music, my surroundings and current events, I approach each new project with the intent to chart a common ground between client needs and the experimental editorial aesthetic. ---
